The first steps are the most difficult

The localities in Romania have started to offer free parking in the public parking lots of the mayorships for 100% electric vehicles and PHEV. The conditions vary from city to city, but in this guide we want to provide the information needed to access such a facility.

We want to see such initiatives in as many localities as possible.

Constant

According to HCL no. 227/2019 for the completion of HCL no. 221/2017 regarding the approval of tariffs for the payment of some services of public utilities, the Local Council of Constanța city has established that “The natural or legal persons from Constanța municipality, users of hybrid or electric vehicles, benefit from gratuity, for the use of the parking spaces from public car parks with hourly payment, from the territory of Constanța municipality. The identification of these vehicles will be done through a vignette, issued by the City Hall of Constanța. ''

An application will be submitted to which the following documents will be attached in copy:

  • Identity card or fiscal registration certificate
  • GDPR statement - can be found on the website of Constanta City Hall
  • Certificate of registration of the vehicle
  • Documents that prove the right to use the vehicle.

These documents can be:

  • also sent to the e-mail address: parcari@primaria-constanta.ro
  • submit at the headquarters of the Management of Public Services, Ștefan Mihăileanu street, no. 10
